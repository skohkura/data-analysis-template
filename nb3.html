From Wikipedia, we see that "The UK has always been the biggest foreign investor in the US and vice versa." (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_Kingdom%E2%80%93United_States_relations). This is referred to as the "special relationship" between the US and UK.¶
Japan's near-zero correlation with the US is unexpected, since the two countries are strong economic partners. This can be explained by Japan's economic difficulties over the two years (http://qz.com/198458/zombies-once-destroyed-japans-economy-now-theyre-infecting-chinas/)¶
